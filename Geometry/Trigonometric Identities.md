## 1 · Core Relationships

### Quotient Identity

$$\tan \theta = \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}$$

### Reciprocal Identities

$$\csc \theta = \frac{1}{\sin \theta} \qquad \sec \theta = \frac{1}{\cos \theta} \qquad \cot \theta = \frac{1}{\tan \theta} = \frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}$$

---

## 2 · Pythagorean Identities

> The other two come from dividing the Golden Rule by $\sin^2\theta$ or $\cos^2\theta$

###  The Golden Rule

$$\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$$

### Tan & Sec

$$1 + \tan^2 \theta = \sec^2 \theta$$

### Cot & Csc

$$1 + \cot^2 \theta = \csc^2 \theta$$

---

## 3 · Negative Angles (Even/Odd)

> Critical for clockwise vs. counter-clockwise rotation

### Even — absorbs the negative

$$\cos(-\theta) = \cos \theta$$

### Odd — pulls the negative out

$$\sin(-\theta) = -\sin \theta \qquad \tan(-\theta) = -\tan \theta$$

---

## 4 · Sum & Difference Formulas

> Foundation for complex number multiplication and 2D rotation

### Sine — middle sign stays the same

$$\sin(A \pm B) = \sin A \cos B \pm \cos A \sin B$$

### Cosine — middle sign flips

$$\cos(A \pm B) = \cos A \cos B \mp \sin A \sin B$$

### Tangent

$$\tan(A \pm B) = \frac{\tan A \pm \tan B}{1 \mp \tan A \tan B}$$

---

## 5 · Double Angle Formulas

> Used when an angle is multiplied by $2$

### Sine

$$\sin(2\theta) = 2\sin\theta\cos\theta$$

### Cosine — 3 variations, use whichever cancels what you need

$$\cos(2\theta) = \cos^2\theta - \sin^2\theta$$ $$\cos(2\theta) = 2\cos^2\theta - 1$$ $$\cos(2\theta) = 1 - 2\sin^2\theta$$

### Tangent

$$\tan(2\theta) = \frac{2\tan\theta}{1-\tan^2\theta}$$

---

## 6 · General Triangle Laws

> For any triangle — sides $a, b, c$ opposite angles $A, B, C$

### Sine Rule

$$\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C}$$

### Cosine Rule

$$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C$$

> Rearrange to find any angle when all 3 sides are known.
